The Story of Banana Ball

Why Banana Ball Started

Banana Ball was created to make baseball more fun, faster, and more exciting for fans who thought traditional baseball was too slow. The goal was simple: turn every game into a nonstop entertainment experience with music, dancing, and wild rules that keep fans engaged every second.

The Banana Ball Teams

Savannah Bananas

Founded in 2016 in Savannah, Georgia. The original Banana Ball team. Known for starting the entire movement and bringing entertainment baseball to life.

Party Animals

The main rival team of the Bananas. Known for wild energy, chaos, and nonstop fun during games.

Firefighters

A high-energy touring team built to bring intensity and entertainment to every Banana Ball game they play.

Texas Tailgaters

A Texas-based Banana Ball team inspired by tailgate culture, food, music, and big stadium energy.

Loco Coco Coconuts

A fun, tropical-themed team built for entertainment, dance, and crazy in-game performances.

Indianapolis Clowns

A historic-inspired Banana Ball team name paying tribute to baseball entertainment history and show-style play.
